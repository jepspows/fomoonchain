dear collector,

this is a letter about Fear Of Missing Outline — a collection of 10,000 hand-drawn characters. one black marker. printer paper. zero erasers. no second drafts. just pure, unfiltered, slightly crooked line art that somehow made it onto the blockchain.

the rules:

  • — one black marker, no colors, no cheating
  • — drawn on standard printer paper, nothing fancy
  • — no erasing. if a line goes wrong it stays wrong
  • — mouths are optional and mostly forgotten
  • — eyes may be different sizes, that's character
  • — arms might be too short, too long, or completely missing
  • — if the marker runs out mid-drawing, the drawing ships as-is
  • — every outline is a little "off" and that's what makes it beautiful

the traits:

  • — 5 heads. mini, small, long, normal, and big. one looks like a potato (affectionate)
  • — 5 ear types. two kinds of human ears, horns (0.4%), elf ears (1%), and dwarf ears (3%)
  • — 9 noses and 9 mouths. well, 9 mouths are available but see rule #4 above
  • — 10 eye types. angry, pretty, normal, sad, crying, happy — and the kid found some colored markers for just the eyes. red (0.2%), blue (2%), green (4%)
  • — 9 hairstyles. from big curly to spicky. some look like actual bird nests
  • — 4 backgrounds. mostly plain white, sometimes gray, cream, or a rare blue (0.6%)
  • — horns (47), elf ears (102), dwarf ears (303), red eyes (16). good luck finding them
  • — no two alike. if you somehow find a duplicate the kid will draw you a personal apology note
