Fear Of Missing Outline
(yes it spells FOMO)
somewhere, 2026
dear collector,

this is a letter about Fear Of Missing Outline — a collection of 10,000 hand-drawn characters made by a kid who may or may not know what they're doing. black marker. white paper. no erasers. no second drafts. just raw, unfiltered, slightly crooked little creatures that somehow made it onto the blockchain.

it started on a rainy tuesday. the kid was bored. the ipad was dead. the tv remote was missing. so they grabbed a black marker and the nearest piece of paper and just... drew. first drawing: a round head. two dot eyes. no mouth. arms coming straight out of the head. the dad looked at it and said "that's actually kind of fire."

next day: another drawing. then another. soon there was a stack of 50. then 100. then the dad had an idea that was either brilliant or very stupid. now there are 10,000 of them. each one unique. each one imperfect. no AI. no copy-paste. every single character is drawn from scratch by a real human child with a real dried-out marker on real paper.

the rules:

  • — one black marker, no colors, no cheating
  • — drawn on standard printer paper, nothing fancy
  • — no erasing, if a line goes wrong it stays wrong
  • — mouths are optional and mostly forgotten
  • — eyes may be different sizes, that's character
  • — arms might be too short, too long, or completely missing
  • — if the marker runs out mid-drawing, the drawing ships as-is
  • — every outline is a little "off" and that's what makes it beautiful

the numbers:

10,000 total supply  |  4,000 whitelist spots  |  0.001 ETH mint price  |  50 max per wallet

rarity:

Common (~60%) regular kid energy, one or two mistakes, still very lovable.
Rare (~25%) marker slipped, extra limb, questionable proportions.
Epic (~12%) multiple errors, forgot a major body part, dad laughed.
Legendary (~3%) absolute chaos, nobody knows what it is, kid was probably tired.
